Convertisseur de Fuseaux

Convertissez date et heure entre fuseaux horaires IANA. Affiche décalages, heure d'été et jour de la semaine.

Saisissez une entrée ci-dessus pour voir le résultat.

How it works

Uses the browser's Intl.DateTimeFormat with IANA time-zone IDs (e.g. Europe/Bratislava, America/New_York). DST transitions and historical offsets are handled by the underlying ICU/CLDR data the browser ships with.

Tips

  • Pick the zone where the time was recorded as the source — DST is resolved automatically.
  • "Now" snaps the input to current local time and source to your browser's zone.
  • Output shows local-format date, the timezone abbreviation, and UTC for unambiguous storage.
